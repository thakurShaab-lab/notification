Teams-style · Works when browser is closed

⚡ How background delivery works
When your server sends a push, it goes to Google/Apple/Microsoft's push servers — not your browser directly. Those servers wake up the Service Worker on your device and show the OS notification, even if the browser is completely closed.

The Service Worker must be registered once (below). After that, notifications arrive as long as the device is online.
📱 iOS: You must install this as an app first
  1. Tap the Share button ⎙ in Safari's toolbar at the bottom
  2. Scroll and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  3. Open the app from your Home Screen
  4. Now enable notifications below — it will work in the background
